Who we are

St Theresas Child & Youth Care Centre, we believe in the power of love, safety, and creating opportunities.

Our Centre is more than an infrastructure — it’s a home where children rediscover their self-worth and begin to heal, one day at a time. As a registered non-profit and public benefit organisation (NPO & PBO), our mission is rooted in service, not in profit. We provide twenty four hour care, rehabilitation, and development to 63 boys only, ranging in age from 4 to 18 years. Each individual has been placed with us through a thorough and caring process, overseen by the Department of Social Development, the Department of Justice, and other dedicated child welfare agencies. On the 6 November 1891, the first Augustinian Sister’s arrived at Estcourt. They began their Apostolate with the education of the young. Since 1925 we salute the founders of the home, the St Augustine Sisters for their charism of “love and mercy” which is foremost in the work done with the boys. We thank them for the love, support and prayers over the past 100 years. For over a century, we’ve continued thatlegacy.


This year marks the celebration of St Theresa’s Home 100th Year Anniversary. “A Century of commitment”. We would like to acknowledge the incredible journey we shared as a community outreach. Every milestone, every challenge we have overcome is a testament to the dedication and talent of each donor and volunteer.


Over the years St Theresa’s Home has evolved, which is evident in the employment of professional child and youth care team and commitment by the board of management. The Child and Youth Care Profession involves and drive the work force of competency, compassion and commitment. All our efforts are underpinned by love and care with God at the centre of it all. The children are our first priority.Every child who comes into our care is received with care, respect, their dignity is upheld and the commitment of development by the childcare team. We’re proud to be part of their journey, offering not only a place of safety, but a place to belong and self-worth. Behind every smile is a story, and behind every story is a community committed to care, dignity, and creating opportunities.

Our Mission Statement

St Theresa's Home provides residential care for children up to the age of 18. Our aim is to provide a loving, caring environment in which the whole child can be nurtured and in which the developmental needs of the children can be met in an appropriate and culturally, sensitive manner by qualified and competent staff. All our work at St Theresa's is underpinned by the Augustinian charisma of mercy.
